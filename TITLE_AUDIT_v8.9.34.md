# Title Conflict Audit — Lorespan v8.9.34

This is a practical publication-risk review, not a legal opinion. Individual titles are generally not protected by copyright, but titles can create trademark, passing-off, platform, or marketplace-confusion concerns.

## Renamed

- **Hero Academia → Halcyon Vanguard** — changed because it is strongly and confusingly similar to the established *My Hero Academia* franchise.
- **Guild Bound → The Ashen Circlet** — changed because an existing fantasy Webtoon uses the exact title *Guild Bound*.
- **Beastbound Rebirth → Wildkin Reawakening** — changed because a 2026 ebook uses the close title *Cursed Rebirth of the Beastbound*.
- **Veilwood Hollow → Gloamroot Vale** — changed because *Veilwood Hollow* is used as a named event zone in *Pet Simulator 99*.

## Retained after exact-title searches

- Tides of the Shattered Crown
- Starfall Archive
- Moonwake Covenant
- Emberwake Court
- Ironhaven Ledger

No clear exact-title commercial conflict was found in the searches performed on 2026-07-18. This does not replace a formal trademark clearance search by an attorney.

## Technical preservation

Internal keys such as `hero`, `guild`, `beastbound`, and `veilwood` were retained. Story routes, choices, variables, callbacks, local-storage/save keys, and branch structures were not renamed or removed.
